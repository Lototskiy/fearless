Social Media Policy

Fearless Futures welcomes feedback or questions about our learning materials and social media posts. If you are interested, we may offer an opportunity for dialogue as long as it is based in Fearless Futures’ principles of engagement when it comes to inclusion discussions: growth, respect, rejecting the binary of a ‘good’ and ‘bad’ person, and agreeing to hear one another. You may contact us at organisations@fearlessfutures.org.
